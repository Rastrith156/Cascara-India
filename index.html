Our Story

From Chikkamagaluru's Estates
to Your Cup

Cascara - the dried husk of the coffee cherry - has been enjoyed for centuries in Ethiopia and Yemen, yet remained hidden in India. We set out to change that.

We partner directly with small coffee growers in the Western Ghats, turning what was once discarded waste into a luscious, fruit-forward brew - rich with notes of tamarind, hibiscus, and dry rose.
