Brew Guide

How to Brew Cascara

01
🌡️

Heat Water

Heat water to 85–90°C. Avoid boiling — it can make Cascara bitter.

→
02
🫙

Add Cascara

Use 1 sachet or 2–3 tbsp of loose husk per 300ml of water.

→
03
⏳

Steep

Steep for 5–7 minutes. Longer steeping gives a bolder, earthier cup.

→
04
🍃

Strain & Sip

Strain, add jaggery or honey if desired, and enjoy hot or over ice!
